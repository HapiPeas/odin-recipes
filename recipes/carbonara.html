Carbonara

Homepage

Description

Carbonara is a pasta dish usually made with guanciale, pecorino cheese, an egg mixture and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 100 gram pasta
  • 1 cup grated pecorino cheese
  • 500 gram cubed Guanciale
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 whole egg
  • Black pepper and salt

Steps

  1. Bring 2 liters of water to a boil
  2. Beat eggs, cheese and pepper together
  3. Cook guanciale on pan on high until cooked, then turn off heat
  4. Put pasta into the water when on a rolling boil and cook until wanted doneness
  5. Once pasta is done, move pasta and pasta water onto pan with guanciale and the egg mixture.
  6. Turn heat to low and cook until the egg mixture thickens into a sauce but before it becomes scrambled eggs
