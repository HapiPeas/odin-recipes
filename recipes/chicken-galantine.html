Chicken Galantine

Homepage

Description

Chicken galantine is a whole chicken that has beheaded, deboned, and usually stuffed with rice, spinach and mushrooms. The chicken is then tied, pan-fried until crispy and finished off in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 500 gram raw spinach
  • 500 gram mushroom
  • 500 gram rice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Fahrenheit
  2. Debone chicken and remove head
  3. Cook spinach, rice and mushroom separately from each other
  4. Stuff chicken with spinach, rice and mushroom
  5. Tie with butcher's twine
  6. Heat up pan with oil and pan-fry chicken until skin is crispy
  7. Place chicken on oven pan until chicken reaches 165 Fahrenheit
  8. Remove and cut into 1-inch slices
  9. Serve
